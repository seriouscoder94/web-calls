Email Success Tips

Before Sending:

  • Research their business thoroughly
  • Check their social media presence
  • Look up recent reviews
  • Find relevant local news
  • Identify their main competitors

Personalization Points:

  • Reference specific products/services
  • Mention local events/news
  • Comment on recent reviews
  • Note business changes/updates
  • Include competitor insights

Follow-up Strategy:

  • Wait 3-4 business days
  • Reference first email
  • Add new value/insight
  • Keep it shorter each time
  • Maximum 2-3 follow-ups
